Top Reconstruction Group

Convenors: Steffen Korn and Tom Stevenson

This twiki is outdated! The new documentation can be found https://top-reco.docs.cern.ch/

Introduction

The principal top reconstruction mailing list is used for meeting, tutorial, and other announcements, and general discussion on top reconstruction issues: hn-atlas-top-reconstruction

You can also use our mattermost chat: invite link and go to the AnalysisTopSupport channel

Top reconstruction meetings every Thursday at 14:00. The actual schedule on Indico can be found at the following address: Top Meeting Calendar

The top reconstruction group has a formal mandate.
