Mandate

The calibration studies cover the optimization of the cluster level corrections applied for the energy measurement of electron and photons, the studies with data of the input variables used in the energy measurement, the derivation of the data driven energy scale and resolution corrections, their checks with different physics samples and the estimation of the systematic uncertainties on energy scale and resolution for electrons and photons.

Single particle ntuples, usually used to train MVA calibration, are ofter available on eos: /eos/atlas/atlascerngroupdisk/perf-egamma/calibration/ntuples_mccalib (mc16 / mc20 / mc23).
