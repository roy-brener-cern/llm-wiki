ATLAS Standard Model Working Group

Convenors: Yusheng Wu and Eram Rizvi

Highest-mass dijet event recorded in 2015. Taken from ATLAS-PHO-Event-2015-059. Standard Model Summary Plot on Measured Cross-Sections. Di photon event recorded in 2011. Interpretation of W boson mass in the view of the Global Electroweak Fit. Taken from ATLAS-STDM-Event-2014-18

This page summarizes important information of the ATLAS Standard Model group. The group focusses of physics processes involving W bosons, Z bosons, photons, jets as well as low energy QCD phenomena, predicted and described within the Standard Model. We currently have five sub-groups (W/Z Physics, Electroweak Physics, Photons + Jets, Soft QCD and Diffractive Physics, the PDF Forum) and more than 400 active ATLAS physicists.

If you are interested to contribute with the Standard Model physics program of ATLAS, please do not hesitate to contact the group conveners for current opportunities to understand the Standard Model in a new energy regime: Yusheng Wu and Eram Rizvi or email atlas-phys-sm-conveners.
